WalkMo Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: April 29, 2026

This page explains how you can delete or manage data related to the WalkMo mobile application ("WalkMo"). WalkMo does not create user accounts and does not operate a developer-managed backend server, so there is no WalkMo account to delete.

Delete Local App Data

WalkMo stores app preferences and app progress locally on your Android device. This may include settings such as theme, unit system, selected app language, whether Health Connect sync is enabled, and ad-earned credits.

You can delete this local data by using any of these options:

  • Use WalkMo's in-app reset option if available for the data you want to remove.
  • Clear WalkMo's app data in Android Settings.
  • Uninstall WalkMo from your device.

Android backup or device transfer features may restore app data if those features are enabled on your device. You can manage Android backup settings in your device settings.

Delete Health Connect Records

If you enabled Health Connect sync, WalkMo may write simulated steps and distance records to Health Connect. WalkMo does not read Health Connect data.

You can review, manage, or delete Health Connect records through Android Health Connect settings. The exact steps may vary by Android version and device manufacturer, but Health Connect controls are generally available in your Android device settings.

You can also revoke WalkMo's Health Connect permissions at any time in Health Connect settings.

Manage Advertising Data Controls

WalkMo uses Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK to show ads. Google may process advertising-related data under Google's own policies.

You can reset or limit use of your Android Advertising ID in Android settings where available. You can also review Google's privacy controls and advertising settings through Google's services.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy and Data

Account Deletion

WalkMo does not provide account deletion because WalkMo does not create or maintain user accounts.
